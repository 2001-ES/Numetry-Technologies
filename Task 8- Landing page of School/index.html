Welcome to ABC School, BAREILLY


The History of ABC school, known for its academic excellence, dates back to the pre - independence era. The First school of the series came into being in the year 1946 under the patronage of Shri Banwari Lal Agarwal ji, a leading lawyer of his time. He dedicated the school in the name of his father (Late) Rai Sahib Lala Gulab Rai ji. The ABC School started off in a very humble manner with just two rented rooms. Today the group can boast of having four esteemed institutions in the city Despite the growth of its infrastructure and facilities, its basic ideals and principles have always remained a source of inspiration. The school is proud of having produced hundreds of eminent persons .
